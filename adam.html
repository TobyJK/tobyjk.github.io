Computer science and its impacts on fincanical technology:

Computer Science has had an emense impact on the way that we make payments.

Within just a few years the way that we make payments has changed dramaitcally. Carrying cash is no longer needed thanks to advances in contactess payment technology such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Contactless payment systems such as Apple Pay and contacless credit cards make use of RFID and NFC technology to allow users to make payments directly from there phone and other smart enabled devices.
